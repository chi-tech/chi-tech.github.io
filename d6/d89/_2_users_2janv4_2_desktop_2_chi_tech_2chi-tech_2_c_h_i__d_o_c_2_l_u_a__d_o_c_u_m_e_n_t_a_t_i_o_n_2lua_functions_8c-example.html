Initializes the Pi's UART serial communication.

Parameters
baudrateint Can be any of the Serial Options.

Serial options

PI3_BAUD_1200 0 PI3_BAUD_2400 1 PI3_BAUD_4800 2 PI3_BAUD_9600 3 PI3_BAUD_19200 4 PI3_BAUD_38400 5

CHI_PI3 chipie; chipie.InitializeSerial(PI3_BAUD_9600);
