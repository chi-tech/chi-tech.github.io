9/**Initializes the data structures necessary for interpolation. This is
10 * independent of the physics and hence is a routine on its own.
11 *
12 * The first step of this initialization is to determine which cells
13 * are intersected by this line. For polyhedrons this is evaluated
14 * face side-by-side.
15 *
16 * The second step is to find the cell associated with each point in
17 * in the line.
18 *
19 * Third step is to upload node indices for each-cell-point pair so that
20 * the value can be interpolated.*/
