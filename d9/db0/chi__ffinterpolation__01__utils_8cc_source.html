12 /** This functions checks the intersection of a plane with a tetrahedron.
13  * The equation of a plane is
14  * nx(x-x0) + ny(y-y0) + nz(z-z0) = 0
15  * Where the plane normal is (nx,ny,nz) and the plane point is (x0,y0,z0).
16  * If we form a dot product between the normal and a vector
17  * (x-x0,y-y0,z-z0) then sign of the result gives the sense to the surface.
18  * Therefore, if we encounter differing senses then the plane is indeed
